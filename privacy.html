Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Elite Automotive Collision Repair ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website, how we use it, and the choices you have. This is a starting template — please have it reviewed by qualified legal counsel before publishing.

Information we collect

When you submit our estimate request form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Vehicle details and a description of the damage
  • Any photos you choose to upload

We may also collect limited, anonymous analytics data (such as pages visited and general location) to understand how our site is used.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your estimate request and contact you about your vehicle
  • To coordinate repairs and, where applicable, communicate with your insurance carrier
  • To improve our website and services

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only as needed to provide our services — for example, with your insurance carrier when you ask us to handle a claim, or with service providers who help us operate (such as our form or hosting providers). We may disclose information when required by law.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations, and we take reasonable measures to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your choices

You may request that we update or delete your information at any time by contacting us. You can opt out of analytics tracking using your browser's privacy settings.
